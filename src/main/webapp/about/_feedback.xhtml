The development of the NURSA 3.0 website has been intimately tied to input emerging directly from the research community that the NURSA Hub serves. The NURSA Beta Testing Group has been instrumental in shaping the look and feel of the site, determining what data are displayed, how they are displayed, and identifying additional data resources that should be included. We thank our beta testers, acknowledged individually below, who attended monthly webinars where new features were demonstrated, navigated the beta site, and provided feedback.
