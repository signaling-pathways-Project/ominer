Getting the Flash Plug-in

Your browser currently does not contain the Macromedia Flash plug-in that is needed to view GeneEd courses. You will need Flash version 5.0.41 or higher. Your browser may try to automatically install it at this point. If it doesn't, please take a moment to download it by clicking on the icon.

Please follow the directions on the Macromedia download page exactly. You may have to download an installation program, quit out of your browser, and then run the program.

If you have downloaded and installed the Flash 5.0 ActiveX control for an Internet Explorer browser and are still having trouble viewing the course, you may need to check and adjust your security preferences.

In Internet Explorer 5:

  1. Select the "Internet Options..." choice from the "Tools" drop-down menu.
  2. Click the Security tab.
  3. Select the Internet zone.
  4. Adjust the security level to Medium or Internet.
  5. Click "OK".
